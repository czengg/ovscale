Tests, Calibrations & More

OV Scale is a one-stop-shop for keeping your scales and balances and instruments in top working order. We can also manage your calibration schedule to make sure your devices are always functioning at their best. With ISO/IEC 17025 accreditation, we meet the most stringent quality standards.

Our highly skilled technicians perform calibrations, testing or repairs onsite or in our climate-controlled lab, and we work closely with you schedule maintenance during downtimes. To make sure we meet your high standards every time, our technicians receive extensive training on all relevant OSHA regulations, including a 10-hour program in which our sales and service representatives also participate.

The services we offer include:

  • 24/7 Emergency Repairs

  • Extreme Capacity Tests

  • Instrument Calibrations

  • Lab Balance Calibrations

  • Preventive Maintenance Contracts

  • Repairs

  • Scale & Balance Calibrations

  • Scale Rentals

  • Turnkey Installations

  • Weight Rentals for Crane Load Testing

The types instruments we calibrate include:

  • Scales

  • Lab Balances

  • Temperature Controllers

  • Pressure Gauges

  • Calipers

  • Micrometers

  • Height Gauges

  • Torque Wrenches

  • Ovens

  • Furnaces

  • Flow Meters

For your convenience, we can store your Certificates of Calibration on our secure, password-protected website. This value-added service allows you to view the complete calibration history for each of your devices, as well as download or print Certificates as-needed.

Whether you're in transportation, manufacturing, processing or mining, OV Scale is ready to help you make sure your scales, weighing systems, instruments and related systems always deliver optimal performance.
